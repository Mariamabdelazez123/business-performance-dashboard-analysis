# 📊 Business Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Power BI solution to analyze overall business performance through multiple interactive dashboards.  
The goal is to go beyond basic KPI tracking and provide a complete data-driven story to support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall business performance across multiple dimensions  
- Identify high-performing regions, products, and customer segments  
- Understand customer behavior and revenue contribution  
- Evaluate the impact of operational factors such as shipping duration  
- Perform scenario analysis to assess discount strategies  

---

## 🗂️ Dashboards Included
- 📈 Sales Dashboard  
- 👥 Customer Analysis Dashboard  
- 📦 Product Performance Dashboard  
- 🌍 Regional Analysis Dashboard  
- ⏳ Time Trend Dashboard

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Technology category generated the highest sales and profit  
- Corporate segment contributed the highest revenue  
- West region achieved the best overall performance  
- Sales trends vary across different time periods  
- Shipping duration impacts customer experience and performance  

---

## 💡 Scenario Analysis (Discount Impact)
A discount strategy of up to 50% was applied to evaluate its effect on business performance:

- **Total Sales:** 14.92M  
- **Total Profit:** 1.52M  
- **Shipping Cost:** 107.83K  

---

## ⚙️ Features
- Interactive dashboards with filters (Region, Segment, Product, Time)  
- KPI cards for performance tracking  
- Multi-dimensional analysis (Customers, Products, Regions, Time)  
- Animated bar chart for dynamic trend visualization

## 🎯 Conclusion
This project focuses on transforming raw data into actionable insights, enabling better business decisions and performance optimization through advanced data visualization and analysis.
